About Us

SafePoint is a community-focused platform designed to make reporting and responding to incidents faster, safer, and more organized. Our mission is to empower individuals to share critical information while keeping their safety a top priority.

Founded in 2025, our team is committed to enhancing public safety through innovative technology, real-time alerts, and a simple, user-friendly system.

How It Works

SafePoint makes reporting incidents simple. These four steps guide how reports move through our system.

1

Submit a Report

Provide details such as location, description, and optional images to begin the process.

2

Initial Review

Reports undergo quick automated checks to filter spam and ensure clarity.

3

Publish & Notify

Valid reports are published on the map and visible to the community.

4

Stay Updated

Users near the incident get notifications and can follow updates in real-time.

Safety Tips

  1. Personal Awareness
    • Stay alert when walking alone, especially at night.
    • Avoid using headphones at full volume outdoors.
    • Keep valuables out of sight in public areas.
  2. Online Safety
    • Never share your exact home location publicly.
    • Avoid posting photos that reveal sensitive details.
    • Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
  3. Emergency Preparedness
    • Save local emergency hotline numbers on your phone.
    • Learn basic first aid techniques.
    • Have a small emergency kit at home.
